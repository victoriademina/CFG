Donna Strickland

Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 2018 for the invention of chirped pulse amplification. She is currently chair of their Presidential Advisory Committee. In 2018, she was listed as one of BBC's 100 Women.

Karen Uhlenbeck

Won the 2019 Abel Prize for "her pioneering achievements in geometric partial differential equations, gauge theory, and integrable systems, and for the fundamental impact of her work on analysis, geometry and mathematical physics."

Ada Yonath

The first Israeli woman to win the Nobel Prize out of ten Israeli Nobel laureates, the first woman from the Middle East to win a Nobel prize in the sciences, and the first woman in 45 years to win the Nobel Prize for Chemistry.
